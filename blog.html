Letter For Bebi

My Letter to Hannie

Hello bebi Hannie, thank you for being patient with me! You trully are my inspirations on things that I persevere in doing right now, syempre third kay Lord, second sa parent. I have a lot to say for our 1 year long relationship, I am really weak at documenting things HAHAHAHAHHAA. Kaya I wanted to make this website to remind us na we have our UPS and DOWNS sa relationship natin, and I really enjoy every second I spent with you. Even though we have our di pagkaka-intidihan madalas, I'm very glad ikaw yung girlfriend ko right now and kung papalarin, by God's grace my partner for the rest of my life.


Back then, I experienced bad things sa past na pinursue ko, and in the end I learned to protect myself by not caring anymore. But, with you I once reminded that there are people like you in this world. You reminded me of how pure people can be. Trully you are my sunshine and the Lord give light. Yung sinukuan kong pangarap of having a wife na pretty, mabait, smart, understanding, caring, giver, not selfish, super bait, and loving is abot kamay ko na sa iyo. I trully love you Hannah, I'm looking forward to the future with you and if not, I will still thank the Lord for showing me how amazing He is through His creation which is you.


I love you bebi, please never lose hope kasi kay Jesus palang panalo na tayu! If you think na wala ka nang kakampe and everyone is your enemy always remember the Lord. He is permanent and He only gave me sayu, you can rely on me sometimes but I cannot guarantee that I'm always there, I surrender what I have to the Lord. I love you bebi more years to come sa iyo and sa atin, and let's pray and read the bible and trust the Lord in the process which He is preparing us. I love you Bebi!
